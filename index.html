About Commonplace

Commonplace is a publication of Ecotrust focused on stories of home. Our hope is to create places for connection, for mapping out new understandings of what is common across states, nations, and human nature. We look forward to hearing your stories about the places you live, your experiences of the stories we publish, and your ideas about how to evolve Commonplace in future issues. Share your thoughts on Facebook, Twitter, or write to us at info@commonplacemagazine.org
